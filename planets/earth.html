Our Blue Planet

Earth is our home and the only known planet to harbor life. The third planet from the Sun, it has the perfect conditions for life with liquid water, a protective atmosphere, and moderate temperatures. Earth's one natural satellite, the Moon, helps stabilize our planet's climate and rotation. Approximately 71% of Earth's surface is covered by water.
