The Gas Giant

Jupiter is the largest planet in our Solar System, a massive gas giant over 11 times wider than Earth. Named after the king of the Roman gods, Jupiter's most famous feature is the Great Red Spot, a storm larger than Earth that has been raging for at least 350 years. Jupiter has a faint ring system and at least 79 known moons, including the four large Galilean moons.
