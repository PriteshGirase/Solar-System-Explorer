The Swift Messenger

Mercury is the smallest planet in our Solar System and the closest to the Sun. Named after the Roman messenger god due to its swift movement across the sky, Mercury is a rocky world with extreme temperatures and a thin atmosphere. Despite being closest to the Sun, it's not the hottest planet in our Solar System!
