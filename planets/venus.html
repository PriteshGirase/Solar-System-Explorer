The Morning Star

Venus is the second planet from the Sun and Earth's twin in size, but with a hellish environment. Named after the Roman goddess of love and beauty, it's the hottest planet in our Solar System with a thick, toxic atmosphere of carbon dioxide. Venus rotates backwards (retrograde rotation) and takes longer to rotate than to orbit the Sun!
