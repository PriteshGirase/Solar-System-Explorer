The Red Planet

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and a primary target for future human exploration. Named after the Roman god of war due to its blood-red appearance, Mars is a cold desert world with the largest volcano and deepest canyon in the Solar System. Evidence suggests that liquid water once flowed on Mars' surface, making it a fascinating candidate in the search for past microbial life.
